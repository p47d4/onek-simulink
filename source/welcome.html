Overview
Onek N.A. Marksmanship Simulink Interface is a web project by OneKobo Technologies designed to manage and display the training data of Nigerian Army Trainees. This interface will help trainees and trainers track progress, analyze performance, and customize training sessions for better marksmanship and combat readiness.

Features
- Weapons Training Data: Detailed records of each traineeâ€™s performance.
- Visuals of Shooting Ranges: Interactive and static visuals to aid training.
- Profile Management: Individual profiles for trainees.
- Customizable Training Ranges: Options to adjust and optimize training environments.
- Support and FAQ: Assistance and common queries for users.

Data Points
1. Stage of Training and Number of Attempts
2. Name of the Shooter
3. Type of Weapon
4. Type of Training
5. Target Number
6. Date of Training
7. Score of Training

The Onek N.A. Marksmanship Simulink Interface is designed to enhance the training experience for Nigerian Army Trainees by providing comprehensive data tracking, customizable training environments, and robust support. This guide should serve as a roadmap for developing a successful and effective interface.
